Both in the academical environment and for my personal growth I engaged myself in numerous projects to make experience and learn. Here are a few examples:

Simultaneous 3D Object Recognition and Pose Estimation System

The objective of the project is to infer the class and the pose of an object, starting from its point cloud representation in the 3D space. The approach is to use a Multi-View Convolutional Neural Network to identify the object. While previous approaches used a fixed number of orthogonal projections of the object to identify, we hypotesized a flexible amount of those point of views would be decrease the computation time required, specially when the object's projections carry a lot of redundant information.

Handwritten Hiragana Characters Recognition

To test a wide array of Machine Learning techniques we used the Kuzushiji-MNIST dataset as a playground. KMNIST is a handwritten characters dataset similar to MNIST, however the character in KMNIST are Japanese characters from the Hiragana alphabet. The project consisted in the development, testing and evaluation of different Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques, starting from very simple algorithms to more complex ensembles of classifiers and Neural Networks. A lot of focus was put on Data Augmentation and Image Processing, and their impact on the models' performance.
